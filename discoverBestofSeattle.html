Sport Events

Seahawks Football

The crown Jewel of Seattle sports. If you haven't been to a game to witness the 12th Man then you haven't lived through a game with more devoted fans. Hawks fans are Loud and Proud!

Mariner's Baseball

This is where legends are born! Ken Griffey Jr, Edgar Martinez, Ichiro, & Robinson Cano, this star studded Seattle History is rich in entertainment. Safeco field is just south of the Downtown area and jam packed with fun things to do before, after, and even DURING the games!

Sounder's Soccer

The newest and hottest team added to the Seattle experience. The Sounders are a force to reckoned with by any team on the home pitch of Century Link Field. With a playoff appearance in every season since its inception in 2009, Seattle has ravaged its opposition with its National team talent, including stars such as MF Clint Dempsey, FW Eddie Johnson and GK Kasey Keller
